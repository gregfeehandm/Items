# Exploding Ammunition

Tags: Item
Town: Baker's Ridge
Last Edited: July 5, 2025 8:10 PM
Attunement: Consumable
Inventory: Dierro

- Choose a point you can see. Each creature in a 20-foot radius sphere centered on that point must make a 13 DC Dexterity saving throw. Any creature in the radius takes 8d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
- If the central point is a creature, make a standard attack roll. If the attack hits, the takes standard arrow in addition to the explosion damage.
- The explosion ignites flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.
- Once this arrow is used, it burns up in the explosion and cannot be recovered.
