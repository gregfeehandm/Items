# Stone of Caustic Infusion

Tags: Item
Continent: Biamer
Kingdom: Ashtengil
Town: Ambersil
Last Edited: July 20, 2025 9:37 AM
Attunement: Attuned
Inventory: Tavion

This small, green-veined crystal hums faintly with transmuted acid essence. When attuned and affixed to a melee weapon, it infuses each strike with a searing caustic force. While attached, the weapon deals an additional 1d6 acid damage on each hit.
