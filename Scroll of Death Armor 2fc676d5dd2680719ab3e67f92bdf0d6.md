### Death Armor

**Level 2 Necromancy (Sorcerer, Wizard)**

**Casting Time:** 1 action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S, M (an onyx gem worth 50+ gp, consumed)

**Duration:** 1 hour

You surround a creature you touch with an aura of chilling, protective shadow.

The target has **advantage on death saving throws**.

Once per turn, when a creature within 5 feet hits the target with a melee attack, the attacker takes **2d4 necrotic damage**.
