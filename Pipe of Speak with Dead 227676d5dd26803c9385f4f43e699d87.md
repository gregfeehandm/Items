# Pipe of Speak with Dead

Tags: Item
Last Edited: July 5, 2025 7:58 PM
Attunement: Not required
Inventory: Felix

Wondrous item, common
This pipe has 3 charges. While smoking it, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to cast the [Speak with Dead](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619068-speak-with-dead?srsltid=AfmBOorkvqOeye3kcqC0ZhSljtEOaFi1x5UrXp815kTafM5pt8e7BoYo) spell from it.

The pipe regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the pipe's last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the pipe reverts to a non-magical pipe.
