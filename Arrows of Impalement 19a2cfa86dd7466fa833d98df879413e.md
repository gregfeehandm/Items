# Arrows of Impalement

Tags: Item
Town: Ambersil
Last Edited: July 5, 2025 8:10 PM
Attunement: Consumable
Inventory: Dierro

On a hit, the target rolls a DC 13 Strength Saving Throw, provided the target is corporeal and on the ground. On a fail, the creature’s speed becomes 0 until your next turn. On a success, it’s speed is reduced by 10 feet.

Once this ammunition is used, it cannot be used again.
