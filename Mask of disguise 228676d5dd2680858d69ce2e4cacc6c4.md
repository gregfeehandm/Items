# Mask of disguise

Tags: Item
Last Edited: July 5, 2025 8:15 PM
Attunement: Attuned
Inventory: Felix

While wearing this mask, you can use an action to cast the [*disguise self*](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Spells:disguise%20self?expansion=34047#content) spell from it at will. The spell ends if the mask is removed.
